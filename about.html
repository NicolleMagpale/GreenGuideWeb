DLSU LPEP Green Guide

The DLSU LPEP Green Guide is a web-and-mobile app that provides information that may be essential to incoming freshmen. With just a few taps, you can access the LPEP schedule of your college.

The Green Guide also shows tips and need-to-knows to make your life in and around DLSU more enjoyable and convenient.
